Club Site

for Chicago Wing Chun

I value my time studying Wing Chun, and the club in Chicago is a great group.

The Chicago Wing Chun Gung Fu Club was founded by Sifu Ken Harnish, student of Sifu Ho Kam Ming, to promote Wing Chun. It is now continued by students of both Sifu Ken Harnish and Sifu Ho Kam Ming. They are a group of Wing Chun enthusiasts that get together to train, share, and learn in a respectful environment. This website was one of my first significant projects, and I enjoyed dusting off my Photoshop skills, too, to resurrect the old club logo.
